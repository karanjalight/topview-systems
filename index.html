Automatic Passenger Counter System

Professional vehicle mounted passenger counter with an AI algorithm. It is able to accurately judge the moving direction of passengers and obtain the precise statistics of passengers boarding and alighting buses.

At the same time, the passenger counter can synchronize the number of passengers and upload the passenger number via network to the monitoring platform to enable analysis & statistical reference of passenger flow data.
